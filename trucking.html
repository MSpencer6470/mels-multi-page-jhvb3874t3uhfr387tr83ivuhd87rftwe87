Trucking Services

Larry Spencer Sr.-President/Ceo-Founder Of Spencer Trucking

Spencer Trucking was founded in July of 2010 by President and CEO, Larry Spencer Sr. Larry started this company
with the vision of creating employment opportunities for many people like himself who suddenly found themselves
without work. Spencer Trucking was started with one truck and trailer and a vision of becoming a leader in the
auto hauling industry. Spencer Trucking has grown over the years, added Spencer Logistics, and now hauls for
several of the major automotive manufacturers along with servicing many companies in the United States and
Canada. We service all 48 of the continental United States and parts of Canada.

Our Mission!

Spencer Trucking was established to achieve the vision of becoming a leader in automotive transportation
services. Dedicated to excellence in the automotive transportation industry, Spencer's Trucking's mission
is to provide outstanding customer service and to deliver products on time, safely, and damage free. At
Spencers the aim is to exceed the customer's expectations. At the same time, Spencer Trucking is also
cognizant of the effect of industry on the environment. Because of this, environmental action to limit
carbon footprint are also an integral part of the company's mission.

Why should Spencer Trucking haul your product?

  • Safety is our number one priority. Spencer drivers follow all federal, state, and local laws.
  • Customer Service is very important to us. Spencer realizes customers need timely deliveries.
  • We use state of the art equipment and processes to load and haul your equipment.
  • We have extensive experience and knowledge in the vehicle hauling business.
  • We provide a professional but personal feel to our business services.

Our Safety Program

Safety is our number one concern at Spencer Trucking. We are dedicated to keeping our drivers, customerâ€™s product,
and the general public safe while on the roads. Spencer Trucking has implemented a safety program to ensure that all
safety measures are followed. Our Safety personell provides training for our current drivers, completes extensive
background checks on potential drivers and sub-haulers, as well as attending many seminars to keep up with the latest
safety regulations.

Our safety department at Spencer Trucking focuses on hiring the best drivers available. This is accomplished by performing
extensive background research using as many resources as possible, such as the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration
(CSA), Motor Vehicle Reports from insurance companies, and scores that are listed on Central Dispatch, just to name a few.
Researching the background of potential drivers extensively ensures that we hire drivers who are skilled in safety regulations.

Spencer Trucking is committed to keeping our drivers up to date on the continually changing safety regulations. Because the D.O.T.
safety rules and regulations are changing continuously, we have put in place a safety department which is responsible for tracking
these changes and implementing training opportunities for our drivers. We ensure our drivers stay up to date on these changes through
regular training programs, safety meetings, and safety checks.

Spencer Trucking has a zero tolerance policy for drivers who do not follow the safety policies and procedures.
