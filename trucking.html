Trucking Services

Larry Spencer Sr.-President/Ceo-Founder Of Spencer Trucking

Spencer Trucking was founded in July of 2010 by President and CEO, Larry Spencer Sr. Larry started this company
with the vision of creating employment opportunities for many people like himself who suddenly found themselves
without work. Spencer Trucking was started with one truck and trailer and a vision of becoming a leader in the
auto hauling industry. Spencer Trucking has grown over the years, added Spencer Logistics, and now hauls for
several of the major automotive manufacturers along with servicing many companies in the United States and
Canada. We service all 48 of the continental United States and parts of Canada.

Why should Spencer Trucking haul your product?

  • Safety is our number one priority. Spencer drivers follow all federal, state, and local laws.
  • Customer Service is very important to us. Spencer realizes customers need timely deliveries.
  • We use state of the art equipment and processes to load and haul your equipment.
  • We have extensive experience and knowledge in the vehicle hauling business.
  • We provide a professional but personal feel to our business services.
