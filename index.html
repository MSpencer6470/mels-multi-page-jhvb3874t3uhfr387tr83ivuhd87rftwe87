Spencer Family Services


Who are we?

As a family growing up in New Orleans,dating back to the 1800's, we faced many problems. As most family do.
Determined to succeed, we faced every obstacle head on. Generation after generation, members of the Spencer
family stepped up to the plate to provide resolutions to all problems. In 2005, Hurricane Katrina snatched up
the Spencers and disbursed them across the United States. In 2022, the youngest son of Dorothy Spencer decided
it was time to bring the family together and offer our services to others. We are now the leading Problem solving
Resolution Company in America. Whatever your problem we have the means to solve it.


What do we do?

Using each of our specific skills, we tackle problems big and small. If you're in the process of getting something
done, and you run into a problem. You give us a call. We have someone for every need. Need products moved? We can
do it! Looking for a new home? Need a job? Those are our most popular services, but we do more! Need a nanny,
homework help, landscaping... We provide it all, Just give us a Call!!


When are we available?

With five generations of Spencers alive and well, we have someone ready to answer your call 24/7.


Where are we located?

We have members of the Spencer family in all areas of the United States. We are working to stretch our servies
across the seas. Whether you need a beach house in California or a delivery in New York, we're there for your
every need. Our base business is in Louisiana, our hometown, but we can be where ever you need us in less than a day.
